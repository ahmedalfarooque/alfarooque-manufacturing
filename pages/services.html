Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom sizes and finishes?
Yes. Wood, steel and aluminium products are manufactured to your project's specific dimensions and finish requirements — this is discussed and confirmed at the quotation stage.
What is your typical lead time?
Lead times vary by project scope, material availability and order volume, and are confirmed when we issue your quotation.
Do you deliver and install, or supply only?
We offer both — supply-only for contractors managing their own installation, or full supply-and-install service where our teams handle delivery and site fitting.
Can I request samples before ordering?
For larger orders, material and finish samples can be arranged — just raise this when you request your quote.
Do you work directly with contractors and developers?
Yes, we regularly work directly with contractors, developers and consultants across all three divisions, from single components to full project packages.
How do I request a quote?
Use the "Request a Quote" button on this page or visit our Contact page — share your project details and a member of our team will follow up.
