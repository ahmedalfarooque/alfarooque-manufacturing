Wood Works

Precision Joinery & Fit-out

Our Wood Works division produces custom joinery, architectural woodwork, fitted furniture, interior fit-out and fire-rated wooden doors using advanced CNC machinery and skilled craftsmen at our dedicated Jeddah factory.

Every product is manufactured to SASO and FD60 fire safety standards — delivering quality that endures.

CNC-routed decorative panels and screens
Veneer work and premium surface finishing

Steel Works

Structural Steel & Fabrication

Our Steel Works division specialises in structural steel fabrication, fire-rated steel doors, metal cladding, staircases, handrails and bespoke steel components for commercial, industrial and residential projects across the Kingdom.

All FD60 fire-rated steel products carry Saudi Civil Defence certification and comply with SASO standards.
