Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship

Since 2000, AL FAROOQUE Manufacturing has been delivering precision manufacturing solutions from Jeddah — part of AL FAROOQUE Industries (IAAE), serving Saudi Arabia and the region.

Who We Are

AL FAROOQUE
Industries (IAAE)

AL FAROOQUE Manufacturing is the manufacturing arm of AL FAROOQUE Industries, operating a Head Office and a dedicated Wood Works Factory in Jeddah. We serve clients across the Kingdom and the region with three core divisions: Wood Works, Steel Works, and Aluminium Works.

Our operations are certified by leading Saudi authorities — including SASO, the Ministry of Industry (MIM), and Saudi Civil Defence (FD60) — ensuring every product meets the highest quality and safety benchmarks.

Fully aligned with Saudi Vision 2030, we invest in advanced manufacturing technology, skilled Saudi talent, and sustainable production methods that support the Kingdom's industrial growth.
